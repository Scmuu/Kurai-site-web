Available Roles

Competitive

The competitive division focuses on ranked ladder climbing, tournaments and scrims. Perfect for players aiming for high ranks in Rocket League.

CCH

Comp Clip Hitter division for ranked players who create highlight clips from their competitive gameplay. Focus on creative editing of ranked moments.

VFX & GFX

Visual Effects and Graphics division dedicated to editing clips, creating profile pictures, banners, thumbnails, and motion graphics for the team.

Freestyler

Freestyle division for players with advanced freestyle skills on Rocket League. Requires high mechanical skill and creativity in aerials, dribbles and flicks.
